Glossary of Terms

TasteBuddies
An app designed to offer personalized restaurant and dish recommendations by matching users with others who share similar taste preferences.
Taste Profile
A personalized profile for each user based on their food preferences, including favorite dishes, spice tolerance, allergies, and dietary restrictions.
Daily Dish
A feed in the app displaying personalized restaurant and dish recommendations, as well as reviews from TasteBuddies with similar taste profiles.
Taste Matching Algorithm
A machine learning-driven system that connects users with similar taste profiles, allowing them to receive tailored recommendations and reviews.
Group Dining API
A feature that analyzes multiple users' taste profiles to recommend restaurants and dishes that suit the entire group's preferences.
SuperTasteBuddies
Users with expert opinions who can provide specialized recommendations on specific foods, acting as influencers within the app.
